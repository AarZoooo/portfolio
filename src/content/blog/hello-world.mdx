work — headings, paragraphs, lists, code, links, blockquotes, all need to land
on the same monochrome aesthetic as the rest of the site.

## What's here

The blog is built on Astro's content collections. Posts live as `.mdx` files
under `src/content/blog/`. Frontmatter is type-checked via a Zod schema in
`src/content.config.ts`, so missing or mistyped fields fail the build.

### What's not here yet

- Per-post OG images
- Tag filtering on the index
- RSS feed
- Search

Those land in follow-up commits as needed.
